What Affects Locksmith Pricing?

1. Time of Day

Emergency and after-hours calls (nights, weekends, holidays) typically cost 25-50% more than standard business hours. If your situation isn't urgent, waiting until morning can save you $25-$75.

2. Type of Lock

High-security locks, smart locks, and commercial-grade locks cost more to service than standard residential locks. A basic Kwikset lock rekey might be $25, while a Medeco high-security lock could be $50+.

3. Vehicle Type

Newer vehicles with advanced key fobs and security systems cost more to unlock and program. Luxury vehicles (BMW, Mercedes, Audi) often require specialized tools and can cost 50% more than standard vehicles.

4. Your Location

Prices in the Greater Toronto Area are typically higher than smaller cities. In the Niagara Region (Welland, St. Catharines, Niagara Falls), prices tend to be 10-20% lower than Toronto.

Detailed Pricing by Service

Car Lockout Service: $75-$150

The cost to unlock your car depends on your vehicle type and time of day. Most standard vehicles can be unlocked for $75-$100 during business hours. Newer vehicles with advanced security may cost $100-$150. This price typically includes the service call fee.

Car Key Replacement: $150-$400+

Replacing a lost or broken car key is one of the more expensive locksmith services. Prices vary dramatically based on:

  • Basic metal key: $50-$100
  • Transponder key: $150-$250
  • Key fob with remote: $200-$350
  • Proximity/push-to-start key: $300-$500

Lock Rekey vs. Lock Replacement

Rekeying changes the pins inside your existing lock so old keys no longer work. It's ideal when you want to keep your current hardware but need new keys (like after moving into a new home).

  • Cost per lock: $20-$40
  • Time: 10-15 minutes per lock

Lock replacement involves installing entirely new lock hardware. Choose this when your locks are damaged, outdated, or you want to upgrade security.

  • Basic lock (Kwikset, Weiser): $80-$150 installed
  • Mid-range lock (Schlage B60N): $120-$200 installed
  • High-security lock (Medeco, Mul-T-Lock): $200-$400+ installed
  • Smart lock installation: $150-$350+ (lock not included)

Why Prices Vary Between Locksmiths

Not all locksmiths charge the same rates, and the cheapest option isn't always the best. Here's what you're paying for with a reputable locksmith:

  • Licensing and insurance - Protects you from damage liability
  • Specialized tools - Professional equipment costs thousands
  • Training and expertise - Proper technique prevents damage
  • Fast response times - 24/7 availability has overhead costs
  • Warranty on work - Peace of mind if something goes wrong

Questions to Ask Before Hiring

  1. What is your total price including service call fee?
  2. Are you licensed and insured?
  3. How long will you take to arrive?
  4. Do you guarantee your work?
  5. What payment methods do you accept?

Lock Change vs. Rekey: Which Do You Need?

Rekeying changes the internal pins so old keys no longer work, but keeps the same lock hardware. It's cheaper ($50-$80 per lock) and works great if your locks are in good condition.

Lock change replaces the entire lock with a new one. Choose this if your locks are old, damaged, or you want upgraded security features like smart locks or high-security deadbolts.

Lock Change Questions Answered

How much does it cost to change locks in Welland?
Lock change in Welland costs $80-$150 per lock for standard locks including labor. High-security locks and smart locks cost $150-$300+. Rekeying is cheaper at $50-$80 per lock if your existing hardware is in good condition.
Should I rekey or change my locks?
Rekey if your locks are in good condition and you just need new keys (after moving, lost keys). Change locks if they're worn, damaged, outdated, or you want upgraded security features. We can inspect your locks and give a recommendation.
How long does it take to change locks?
A single lock change takes 15-30 minutes. Most homes with 2-3 locks are completed within an hour. We offer same-day service and can usually arrive within 30-60 minutes of your call.
Do you install smart locks?
Yes! We install all types of smart locks including August, Yale, Schlage Encode, Kwikset Halo, and more. We can help you choose the best smart lock for your needs and budget.
Can you make all my locks use the same key?
Yes, this is called "keying alike" and is included free when we change or rekey multiple locks. You'll have one convenient key for all your doors.
